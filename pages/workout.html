💪 Workout Routines

Tailored workouts for every fitness level.

🏃 Beginner Plan

  • Jumping Jacks - 2 mins
  • Bodyweight Squats - 3 sets of 10
  • Plank - 30 sec
  • Wall Sit - 45 sec
  • Arm Circles - 1 min each direction
  • Knee Push-ups - 3 sets of 10
  • Standing Calf Raises - 3 sets of 15
  • Glute Bridges - 3 sets of 10
  • Step Touch - 2 mins
  • Seated Toe Touch - 30 sec

🏋️ Intermediate Plan

  • Push-ups - 3 sets of 15
  • Lunges - 3 sets of 12 each leg
  • Mountain Climbers - 2 mins
  • Russian Twists - 3 sets of 20
  • Burpees - 3 sets of 10
  • Tricep Dips (chair) - 3 sets of 12
  • Leg Raises - 3 sets of 15
  • Bicycle Crunches - 3 sets of 20
  • Superman Hold - 30 sec
  • Jump Squats - 3 sets of 15

🔥 Advanced Plan

  • Pull-ups - 3 sets of 8
  • Pistol Squats - 3 sets of 8 each leg
  • Plank to Push-up - 3 sets of 12
  • Jump Squats - 3 sets of 15
  • High Knees - 2 mins
  • Clapping Push-ups - 3 sets of 10
  • Toe Touch V-Ups - 3 sets of 20
  • Jump Lunges - 3 sets of 10 each leg
  • Side Plank (each side) - 45 sec
  • Burpee to Tuck Jump - 3 sets of 6

🧘 Recovery & Flexibility

  • Cat-Cow Stretch - 1 min
  • Child's Pose - 2 mins
  • Hamstring Stretch - 1 min each leg
  • Butterfly Stretch - 2 mins
  • Neck Rolls - 1 min each direction
  • Standing Quad Stretch - 1 min each leg
  • Seated Forward Fold - 2 mins
  • Thread the Needle Stretch - 1 min each side
  • Hip Flexor Stretch - 1 min each side
  • Shoulder Rolls - 1 min

🏠 Home Cardio Blast

  • Jump Rope - 3 mins
  • Skaters - 3 sets of 20
  • Toe Taps on Stairs - 2 mins
  • Shadow Boxing - 3 mins
  • Jump Lunges - 3 sets of 10
  • Fast Feet Shuffle - 2 mins
  • Mountain Climbers - 2 mins
  • Burpees - 3 sets of 15
  • High Knees - 2 mins
  • Running in Place - 3 mins
